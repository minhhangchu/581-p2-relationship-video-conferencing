The Relationship
Create a video-conferencing interface (audio/video) to foster a closer relationship between grandparents and baby. Designed specifically with the COVID-19 pandemic, this interface provides more interactive way for grandparents connecting with their grandchildren.
Date
April 7, 2021
Project 2 - CPSC 581 W2021, University of Calgary
Summary
Rhys is a 8-month-old baby. Due to Covid-19, Rhys cannot meet grandparents (Opa & Oma) in person. Rhys needs to learn Opa & Oma's faces and voices. This video-conferencing interface has live and interactive tools to help Rhys building relationship.
Concept
Starting with concept sketches. 10 varied concept sketches above show different meaningful interactions between Opa & Oma with Rhys. Since Rhys is a baby, all the ideas are toward connecting physical interaction through distance. Interaction includes: reading books, singing, playing games (with assistance from Papa and Momma), going for a ride simulation and more.
Refined sketches & Ideas
Refining ideas by creating over 10 variations of a particular concept. All of the refined sketches are about the idea of connecting Opa & Oma with physical activity with Rhys. Opa & Oma will use a virtual controller to control car/toys/boats, Rhys can ride on it or crawl after it. Sketches below show different interactions and visuals with the above idea, ranging from car simulation, boat simulation and more.
Implementation
This video-conferencing interface was implemented in simple HTML, CSS, and JS, using WebRTC. Opa, Oma, Rhys, Momma will go to the demo link below. Opa & Oma can click on their button, while Rhys, Momma, and Papa can click on their button. After clicking, either can click "Call" to start streaming others' videos. Once the connection is established. Opa & Oma can control the steering wheel and lights on the top left corner to give directions to Momma and Papa, they will follow these directions to interact with Rhys. As the steering wheel moves, the compass will also move to indicate directions Rhys should go better. Momma or Papa is a part of the system.
